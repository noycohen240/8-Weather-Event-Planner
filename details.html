Strawberry Picking - Nissimov Farm

Escape the city and enjoy a fun and tasty experience for all ages at Nissimov Farm! We invite you to come and pick your own fresh, sweet strawberries straight from the field. This is more than just picking fruit; it's a chance to connect with nature, breathe fresh country air, and create lasting memories with your loved ones.

Our fields are carefully maintained to provide the best-tasting strawberries, grown with care and passion. It's a perfect family outing, an ideal activity for a sunny day, and a wonderful opportunity to teach children where their food comes from. Fill your basket with juicy, red strawberries and enjoy the simple pleasure of a day on the farm.

Location: 3 Egozi St, Ramla

Price: Child: 35 ILS | Adult: 60 ILS
